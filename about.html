Who We Are

T&M Destinations is a premium travel and transfer service designed for international students, travelers, and professionals arriving in China. We offer fixed-rate, high-quality airport transfers, and comprehensive travel assistance to ensure a seamless experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide stress-free, reliable transportation solutions and exceptional customer service, ensuring that newcomers to China have a smooth and comfortable arrival experience.

What We Offer

We offer a range of services including premium airport transfers, flight & train booking assistance, hotel reservations, embassy document support, and student registration assistance. Our dedicated team of local assistants ensures that every client receives personalized, high-standard service.
