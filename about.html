Who We Are

T&M Destinations is a premium travel and transfer service designed for international students, travelers, and professionals arriving in China. We provide top-tier airport transfers, accommodation assistance, and local travel solutions to ensure a smooth experience.

Our Mission

We aim to offer stress-free and reliable transportation solutions, ensuring that newcomers can comfortably and efficiently reach their destinations in China.

What We Offer

✅ Fixed-rate airport transfers across major Chinese cities.

✅ Assistance with flight, train, and hotel bookings.

✅ Support with police registration and university check-ins.
