Our Premium Services

  • Airport Transfers: Reliable and safe transportation from all major international airports in China.
  • Airport Pick-Up (Same City): Efficient local transfers for within-city travel.
  • Flight & Train Booking Assistance: Expert help booking flights from your home country to China and arranging train tickets to your final destination.
  • Hotel Booking & Check-In Assistance: Hassle-free support to secure and check into your accommodation.
  • Student Assistance: Comprehensive support for international students, including police registration, SIM card setup, and university check-in.
  • Currency Exchange Assistance: Guidance for smoothly exchanging USD to CNY upon arrival.
  • Tour Guide Services: Premium guided tours for business travelers and tourists. Services begin when you reach the first sightseeing location and end upon return. For full-day tours, clients cover accommodation for both themselves and the guide.
  • Embassy Assistance: Professional support for preparing, certifying, and submitting documents to embassies in Beijing. This service is available for clients from:
    • Zimbabwe
    • South Africa
    • Botswana
    • Tanzania
    • Congo
    • Gabon
    • Kenya
    • Swaziland
    • Mozambique
